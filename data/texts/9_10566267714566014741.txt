Summary:
. No discrete HD bands has been firmly identified yet, but
· Continuum "HD and SD" Ridge Structures identified in 14 nuclei.
· 118,122Te, 124,125Xe, and (124),125Cs strong candidates for Hyperdeformation.
· For 120Te,121,122I, 121,122,123,126Xe, 126Ba, Superdeformation are more likely.
· The length of the ridges indicates 6-10 transitions in many bands.
· Fluctuation Analysis on the best cases shows  10 bands.
· A strong beam energy dependence is observed close to the Lmax .
· Incomplete Fusion may explain the beam energy dependence and the high-angular momentum feeding in the , 2 and 2p channels, Supported by Angular Distibutions of Light Charged Particles with enhanced intensity at angles  45o (A. Pasternak's Modelling.) And it is thereby also easier to understand how (2p,2n) and (2,2n) can populate the very high spin region, needed to see HD/SD. II
